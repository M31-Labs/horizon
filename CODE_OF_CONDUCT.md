# Code of Conduct

Horizon is an engineering project for safe, typed eBPF authoring. Discussions
should stay technical, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Explain tradeoffs with concrete examples.
- Keep feedback focused on code, design, tests, documentation, and security.
- Assume reports may come from people with different kernel, Go, or eBPF
  experience.
- Avoid sharing private source, logs, credentials, generated artifacts, or host
  details that do not belong in a public issue.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing someone else's private information or security details.
- Derailing technical discussion with repeated off-topic comments.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, open a minimal public issue asking for maintainer contact
without including private details. For suspected vulnerabilities, follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation
when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
